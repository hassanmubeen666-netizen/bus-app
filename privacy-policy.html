Privacy Policy — Bustrip Driver

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Bustrip Driver ("the app") is a mobile app for drivers working with omnibus (long-distance bus) operators who use the Bustrip Fleet platform. This policy explains what information the app collects, why, and how it's handled. It applies only to Bustrip Driver — the separate Bustrip Fleet office dashboard is covered by its own policy.

In short: Bustrip Driver is an internal work tool provided to you by your employer (the fleet operator). Everything you submit — photos, trip details, requests — is visible to your employer's office staff and administrators, for the purpose of running their transport business. It is not sold, and it is not shared with advertisers.

1. Who provides this app

Bustrip Driver is provided to you by your employer — the transport/omnibus company you work for — using the Bustrip Fleet platform. Your login (username and password) is created and managed by your employer's office staff, not by you directly.

2. Information we collect

The app collects the following, only when you actively use a feature:

  • Account information: your username, display name, and a securely hashed password.
  • Photos, taken with your device's camera: a selfie for daily attendance; photos of the bus exterior and interior; photos of LR (Lorry Receipt) paperwork when carrying luggage; optional photos attached to vehicle issue reports.
  • Trip and vehicle data you enter: odometer readings, departure/arrival times, delay reasons, fuel purchases, fare collected, and the vehicle you're reporting on.
  • Requests you submit: leave requests (with dates and reasons) and cash advance requests (with amounts and reasons).
  • Earnings information: your own trip and salary records, as logged by your employer's office, shown back to you inside the app.

The app does not access your contacts, call history, messages, or files unrelated to the features above. It does not track your location in the background.

3. Camera permission

The app requests camera access solely so you can take the photos described above (selfie, vehicle photos, document photos) at the time you choose to use those features. Photos are uploaded directly to your employer's account; the app does not access your device's photo gallery beyond the picture you just took.

4. How this information is used

  • To confirm your attendance and the condition of the vehicle at the start of each journey
  • To let your office reconcile trips, fuel, and fares against what was actually driven and collected
  • To process leave and advance requests
  • To calculate and show your own trip earnings
  • To let your office follow up on reported vehicle issues

5. Who can see this information

Information you submit is visible to your employer's office staff and administrators (the people who manage your company's Bustrip Fleet account) — not to other drivers, not to the public, and not to Bustrip's own team beyond ordinary technical hosting and support. It is never sold, and never shared with third parties for advertising or marketing.

6. Where data is stored

Data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore), a cloud database service. Reasonable technical safeguards are used, including password hashing. As with any employer-provided work tool, access to view or manage this data is controlled by your employer's account administrators.

7. Data retention

Information is retained for as long as your employer's business needs it for operational and payroll records, or until your employer requests its deletion. If you leave your job, contact your employer's office about removing your account.

8. Your choices

Since your account is managed by your employer, requests to access, correct, or delete your data should go through your transport office. They can reach Bustrip support using the contact details below if needed.

9. Children's privacy

This app is a professional tool for employed drivers and is not directed at, or intended for use by, children.

10. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same address with a new "Last updated" date.

11. Requesting account deletion

Your Bustrip Driver account is created and managed by your employer's office, not by you directly — there is no self-service "delete my account" button inside the app itself. To request that your account and associated data (photos, trip reports, requests, earnings records) be deleted:

  • Ask your employer's office/admin to remove your account from the Staff Accounts section of their dashboard, which deletes your login and disables further access; or
  • Email bustripinfo1@gmail.com with your username and company name, and we will forward the request to your employer's administrator.

Deletion requests are typically processed within a few business days. Some records (e.g. completed trip logs tied to your employer's financial/payroll history) may be retained by your employer as needed for their own business and legal record-keeping, even after your login is removed.
