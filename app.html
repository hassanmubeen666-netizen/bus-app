Route Profitability & Loss Recovery

See which routes make money, which routes lose money, and the revenue required on the next run to recover carried-forward losses.

How recovery target works
Bustrip carries forward losses until future profits cover them. Suggested next-run revenue = recent average operating cost + unrecovered loss. The seat-rate planner then divides that target by the expected number of seats sold. EMI daily allocation and averaged statutory costs are included in the operating-cost calculation.
