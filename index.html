Traffic delay proof

Questions

Built to be simple in the yard.

Does the driver need to do many entries?

No. After check-in, the driver only sees Parcel Entry, Traffic Delay Evidence and Speedometer/Odometer.

Can the office verify traffic delay?

Yes. Each delay proof can include photo, GPS coordinates, accuracy, timestamp, delay minutes and reason.

Does it track vehicle profit?

Yes. Daily operations, revenue, expenses and vehicle records are connected to show profit and performance.

Does the app work on mobile?

Yes. The office and driver apps are responsive and built for phones as well as desktops.

Need help setting up Bustrip?

Send your fleet details, vehicle count, and current workflow. We’ll help you get the office app and driver app ready for daily use.
